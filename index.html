Welcome to PIG DIE. This is a fun and intense game first coined by magician John Scarne in 1945.


The rules of the game are simple:

  • A player rolls the die.
  • Whatever side you land on you get those points.
  • If you want you can keep rolling to add up points, or you can hold and end your turn.
  • Be careful! If you land on a 1, your turn ends and you lose any points you wouldve had.
  • First person to add their points up to 100 or above wins!
